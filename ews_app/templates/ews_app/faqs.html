Frequently Asked Questions

What is EWS?
EWS is a deflationary educational-meme token that provides theoretical courses and practical executions within the overall cryptocurrency ecosystem. we are committed to education, knowledge, charity-supporting the care and conservative efforts towards the Whales of the oceans. the EWS DAO reserves a share of the token to support the conservation of these extincting majestic creatures.
How does EWS work?
EWS is a decentralized cryptocurrency that operates on the Binance Smart Chain. It is designed to be deflationary, which means that the total supply of tokens will decrease over time. This is achieved through a mechanism called token burning, where a portion of the tokens are permanently removed from circulation. This will boost demand and rapidly increase market value.
How can I purchase EWS tokens?
You can purchase EWS tokens on decentralized exchanges (DEXs) such as Pancakeswap, Boggedfinance, and Poocoin, please note that the value of EWS tokens may flunctuate, and its important to do your own research and invest wisely.
How can I store EWS tokens?
You can store EWS tokens in any BSC-compatible wallet that supports BEP20. Some popular options include Trust wallet, MetaMask, Safepal etc. It is important to keep your private keys safe, secure and never share them with anyone.
